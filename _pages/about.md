I am currently a freshman undergraduate student pursuing a bachelor's degree in Computer Science at the College of Science, Purdue University West Lafayette.

My research interests primarily focus on Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. I have a keen interest in technologies and applications related to deep learning, natural language processing, and computer vision. I would like to dedicate myself to gaining in-depth knowledge of machine learning algorithms, model optimization, and the practical applications of artificial intelligence. I aspire to contribute to the advancement of technology and innovation in the future through my in-depth research in this field.

This summer, I led a project titled "Motion Prediction through Hand-Drawn Sketches" under the guidance of PhD student Max and Assistant Professor Yexiang Xue. Traditional motion prediction encodes image sequences into latent vectors for processing and prediction. Using hand-drawn sketches instead offers better interpretability and retains essential information while removing redundancy, leading to more accurate predictions. This project is ongoing, and we continue to refine our techniques to improve motion prediction accuracy using sketches..

I also joined the Vertically Integrated Program which is hosted by the Purdue Aerial Robotics Team with the IEEE chapter's PART committee to develop autonomous UAV platforms with computer vision and machine learning applications from January 2024.

Before transferring to Purdue University, I studied Communication Engineering at Northeastern University (China) for one year and a half. During that time, I had the privilege of being mentored by Professor Xiaoyi Luo, starting in March 2023, to dedicate myself to a research project titled "Water Treatment System for Saline Bores." I served as the group leader and achieved the highest evaluation.

I also had the honor of serving as a Teaching Assistant for the Network Fundamentals course under the guidance of Dr. Peng Han in Fall 2023.
